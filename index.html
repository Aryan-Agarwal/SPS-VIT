ABOUT US

IEEE Signal Processing Society VIT Student's Chapter is one of the prominent working chapters of VIT. IEEE SPS Vision statement:"To be a one-stop source of signal processing resources, providing a variety of high quality resources to a variety of users in formats customized to their interests while adapting to a rapidly changing technical community."

IEEE SPS works as a family, helps students working on projects and assignments of related topics can come for assistance, prepare for competitive exams through specialized lectures and workshops, hosts information and counselling sessions to highlight opportunities of signal processing in a career point of view. Thus IEEE SPS keeps members abreast of the latest information and to serve the public at large.

EVENTS

CLASSROOM EVENTS

DIGITAL IMAGE PROCESSING(DIP)


The event is focussed on Image Processing techniques used for editing images in MATLAB. The event comprises of reading an image, converting it to grayscale and processing it for better understanding, reading a colour image and splitting it to RGB Components and processing it. Different kinds of filter like blurring, sharpening, temperature adjustments and edge detection are taught. The purpose of the event is to help people understand the theory by coding it in practical.

Arduino basics and Sensor Interfacing

The different types of communication protocols in Arduino are taught. Sensors like Ultrasonic sensor, IR Sensor, Temperature and Humidity sensor are directly interfaced in the session. Finally all these three sensors are interfaced together for better performance. By the end of the event the participants are also able to interface sensors.

VERILOG


Introduced to the concepts of Digital electronics by bringing an analogy with its counterpart. i.e Analog circuits. Basic usage of digital circuits based appliances in our day-to-day lives are used as reference and modelling are done on Model simulator.

MATLAB


The sole purpose of the workshop is to introduce students to computer vision and get familiarized with a few basic concepts of deep learning and machine learning. What is taught to the students is basically how face recognition leverages computer vision to extract discriminative information from facial images, and pattern recognition or machine learning techniques to model the appearance of faces and to classify them.

PHOTOSHOP


A lot of budding designers participate in this event. The software is provided to all the participants who didn't have Photoshop. All the basic functions of Photoshop like view, select, enhance, draw, modify, Move tool (V), Elliptical Marquee tool (M), Magnetic Lasso tool (L), Selection Brush tool (A), Auto Selection tool (A), Magic Wand tool (A), Layer to later editing, Effects on each layer, Layer mask and Blur effect are taught.

RIVIERA

SPIN IT TO WIN IT


One of the most exciting and fun event of Riviera 2019, Spin it to win it. It is an one on one game in which one participant tries to win over the other knocking over a set of wickets(6 stumps, 3 placed on each side) placed diagonally opposite to them. The pitch will be divided into two equal halves, with the participants playing simultaneously. The participant who manages to spin and knock the wickets more number of times, wins. The participants will be provided with a stumper ball. The catch will be that the participant will have to spin the ball in the specified area and then knock the wickets.

IBM DEV

Internet of Things is the need of the hour which will add as an additional skill for the participants which will be industry oriented.
